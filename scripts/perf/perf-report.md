## Recommendations

- Scroll averaged **33.8 fps** across 13 samples. Top suspects under throttle: `glass-adaptive.js` IntersectionObserver re-sampling each `.card` and the per-card backdrop-filter blur. Try gating IntersectionObserver to one-shot (already done) and adding `content-visibility: auto` to long card lists (companies grid, lesson list).
- Nav averaged **30.5 fps**. Most cost is the synchronous `render()` re-paint of the route's view (full `innerHTML` swap). Splitting heavy renders (companies, dashboard) into a sync skeleton + async chunked fill would smooth the transition.
- Interactions averaged **34.0 fps**. Suspects: lesson modal mount (Prism highlight + activity mount in one pass) and flashcard flip transform with preserve-3d. Consider deferring Prism highlighting until after the modal animates in.
- Single 261.0 ms task during `dashboard:back` blocks all input. Slice it (`requestIdleCallback` chunks, or yield via `await 0`).
